We are a team of researchers, engineers, and designers passionate about understanding human behavior.

We believe that by combining the power of generative AI with the principles of behavioral science, we can create a new generation of tools that will help businesses better serve their customers.

Our Mission.

To build the world's most powerful and accessible platform for simulating human behavior, enabling a deeper and more empathetic understanding of the human experience.

What We Believe In.

Scientific Rigor

We are committed to a first-principles approach, grounding our work in established scientific research.

Responsible Innovation

We are mindful of the ethical implications of our work and are committed to building a platform that is used for good.

Collaboration & Openness

We believe that the best ideas come from collaboration. We are committed to working openly and transparently with our partners and the community.
