What Makes Our Personas Different?

Rich, Generative Backstories

Each persona is generated with a unique life story, including family, career, education, and significant life events. This provides the foundation for their beliefs and behaviors.

Evolving Memory and Beliefs

Our personas have a dynamic memory. They learn from their experiences, form new beliefs, and their opinions can change over time, just like real people.

Sophisticated Emotional Models

Personas have a baseline mood and their emotional state changes in response to events in the simulation. This allows us to model the emotional drivers of consumer choice.

Highly Customizable and Extensible

Define the specific traits, characteristics, and even psychological models that are most relevant to your research questions. The platform is designed to be extensible.
