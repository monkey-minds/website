Simulate Complex Customer Journeys

Model end-to-end customer journeys that span multiple touchpoints, channels, and even long periods of time. Understand how different experiences influence a customer's decisions.

Dynamic and Configurable Environments

Define the world of your simulation. Configure the environment, the available choices, and the external events that can influence your personas' behavior.
